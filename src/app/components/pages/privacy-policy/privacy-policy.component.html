2. If you are under the age of 18

We may check that your parent or guardian has given you such permission, for example by asking you to provide us with your parent's or guardian's contact details so we can contact them. 5. Keeping your information secure

At MakerElec, we prioritize the security and privacy of our users' data. To ensure the utmost protection of your information, we store user data using Microsoft's cloud storage solution, a trusted and industry-leading platform. Below, we outline how Microsoft's cloud storage solution maintains data security:

  • Data Encryption: Microsoft's cloud storage solution employs robust encryption protocols to secure your data during transmission and while at rest. This safeguards your information from unauthorized access and interception.
  • Physical Security: Microsoft's data centers maintain stringent physical security measures, including access controls, surveillance, and biometric authentication, to prevent unauthorized access to their facilities.
  • Redundancy and Backups: Microsoft's cloud storage solution ensures data redundancy and performs regular backups to mitigate the risk of data loss and maintain data availability.
  • Access Controls: Access to user data is tightly controlled, and only authorized personnel have permission to access the data. Microsoft implements role-based access controls and multi-factor authentication to prevent unauthorized access.
  • Compliance and Certifications: Microsoft's cloud storage solution adheres to industry best practices and complies with various security standards, including ISO/IEC 27001, SOC 2, and GDPR, to meet stringent data security and privacy requirements.
  • Data Monitoring: Microsoft employs real-time monitoring and threat detection mechanisms to promptly identify and respond to potential security incidents, ensuring data remains protected.
  • Data Isolation: Your data is stored in isolated environments, ensuring it remains segregated from other users' data, further enhancing data privacy and security.
  • Continual Improvement: Microsoft's cloud storage solution undergoes regular security assessments, audits, and updates to adapt to emerging threats and ensure data protection is continuously improving.

While we entrust Microsoft's cloud storage solution to securely store your data, it is essential to understand that no data storage method is entirely risk-free. We remain committed to maintaining the confidentiality and integrity of your information, and we continuously review our security measures to protect against potential risks.

By leveraging Microsoft's cloud storage solution, we can offer you a reliable and secure service. We value your trust and confidence in our platform and are dedicated to upholding the highest standards of data security and privacy. We store your information so we can complete your order and also to make the option available to you if you wish to reprint your order at a future date or should your initial order be lost or damaged.

We store your name, address, email, and contact number indefinitely.

9. How can I delete my data?

If you wish to have your personal data deleted from our records, please follow the steps outlined below:

Step 1: Submit a Data Deletion Request:

To initiate the data deletion process, you are required to submit a formal request. Please send an email to support@MakerElec from the email address associated with your account. In your request, clearly state your intention to have your personal data deleted and provide any relevant account details or identifiers.

Step 2: Verification of Identity:

To ensure the security and privacy of your information, we will require efficient proof of your identity. This is essential to prevent unauthorized access to or deletion of your data. Upon receiving your deletion request, we will respond with specific instructions on the type of identification documents or information needed for verification purposes.

Step 3: Confirmation of Data Deletion:

Once we have successfully verified your identity, we will initiate the data deletion process.

Types of data subject to deletion:

Upon your successful request, we will delete the personal data that falls under the following categories:

  • Data provided during registration or account creation.
  • Data collected through your interactions with our services.
  • Data obtained through your participation in surveys, contests, or promotions.
  • Communication history and preferences.

Retention of certain information:

Please be aware that certain information may be retained for legitimate business purposes or to comply with legal obligations. This may include transaction records, financial data, and information necessary for record-keeping.
